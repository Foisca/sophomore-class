### The differences between boat and ship

boat, lifeboat, fishing boat,(single deck or without deck 单甲板或者无甲板)

ship, vessel, (several decks 几层甲板)

### Abbreviations

T.S = training ship 训练船

M.V = motor vessel 机动船

M.S = motor ship 机动船

M.T = motor tank 机动油船

S.S = steam ship 蒸汽机船

O.T = Oil tanker 油船
