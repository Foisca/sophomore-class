### The differences between boat and ship

boat, lifeboat, fishing boat,(single deck or without deck 单甲板或者无甲板)

ship, vessel, (several decks 几层甲板)

### Abbreviations

T.S = training ship 训练船

M.V = motor vessel 机动船

M.S = motor ship 机动船

M.T = motor tank 机动油船

S.S = steam ship 蒸汽机船

O.T = Oil tanker 油船

### By means of refinery process 提炼过程 

Product oils are refined from crude oil:

* jet iol 	石油脑，航空燃油
* gas oil     汽油
* disel oil 柴油
* kerosene 煤油
* lubrication oil, lubricant iol, lubricant, lubricating oil, LO 滑油
* fuel oil, FO 燃油
* paints 油漆
* bitumen 沥青

### Locations and Directions

* starboard side 右舵(the right side of a ship)
* catwalk 游步甲板
* port side 坐舵
* bow, forecastle, head 船首
* forecastle deck 艏楼甲板
* shear 舷弧(the forward par and aft part are higher than the middle part)
* camber 梁拱(the nearer to the centre line, the higher of the point)
* stern, quarters, poop decks 船尾，艉楼甲板
* quarters 舿

* IMO number 国际海事组织编号(International Maritime Organization)
* ship's name 船名
* port of registry 注册港
* port of origin 始发港
* port of departure 离港
* port of call 挂靠港
* port of arrival 抵达港
* port of destination 目的港
* port of registry, home port 注册港， 母港
* voyage 航次
